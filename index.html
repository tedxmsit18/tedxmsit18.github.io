2015 Event

TEDxMSIT-2015 was an outstanding and an awe-inspiring event which took place on 20th October 15 in the college campus. It was the first TEDx event in the history of MSIT and it left a remarkable impression among the attendees. A team of 25 diligent members Worked sedulously and painstakingly to make it incredible. In sync with the spirit of TED’s mission, i.e., ideas Worth spreading, the team invited 10 eloquent speakers from different backgrounds to deliver TED talks with their unparalleled public speaking skills. Each of the speakers came up with scintillating, motivating and inspirational ideas to Whir the minds of the audience. The attendees Were flabbergasted With the thought provoking ideas that the speakers came up with. They tried to incorporate the concept of selfless service to promote community based service. The event proved to be phenomenal and it was remarkably appreciated by the Times of India and Millennium Post.

What is TEDx?

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organised events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxMSIT, where x = independently organised TED event. At our TEDxMSIT event, TEDTalks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organised.
